# Product Brief

## Product vision

Coffee Ops Hub is an internal business operations hub for organizing the work required to run the business. It should become the single place to coordinate tasks, goals, decisions, and operational records.

## Audience

- Primary audience: owners first
- Secondary audience: staff later

The first version should be usable and efficient for the owners, while the product structure should remain compatible with later staff access.

## Core problem

Operational work is fragmented across memory, messages, spreadsheets, and ad hoc notes. That creates missed follow-ups, poor visibility, and no reliable shared record of what needs attention.

## First-version intent

Version 1 is a task and communication hub focused on:

- shared tasks
- work-item comments
- shared goals
- owner collaboration
- recent activity visibility

## Planned expansion

Future modules should extend the product into:

- inventory management
- vendor management
- purchasing and purchase orders
- invoice tracking
- operating notes and records
